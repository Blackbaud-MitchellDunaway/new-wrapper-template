TEST PAGE for previewing a PageWrapper.

This is an example of how a page with this PageWrapper will look. You may browse the site using the links that exist in the PageWrapper, and any page on your site that uses this PageWrapper will render with it. All other pages will render with the appropriate PageWrapper for that page.

This current PageWrapper is stored in your session, and if you login (which gives you a new session) you will need to add this PageWrapper back into your session. To do this you add to your URL pw_id=# (where # is the id of your PageWrapper).
